This template is a simple and quick way to build a HTML resume and perfect for showcasing your online resume. It has a fully responsive structure built with Bootstrap 3 and it is retina ready, so it looks stunning on all types of screens and devices. The template is clearly coded and commented for easy to customize to fit your needs, replace images and texts.
